About Us

Welcome to Downsy learners in collaboration with ZRC. We're dedicated to supporting individuals with Down syndrome and their families through awareness, providing resources, and shifting public perception. Our mission is to empower individuals with Down syndrome, challenge stereotypes, and foster inclusivity. Join us in creating a world where everyone, regardless of ability, can thrive!

Our Services

A range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of children with various disabilities. Here are some common services these apps provide

Behavioral and Social Skills Training

Interactive exercises to improve speech articulation and language comprehension. Visual and auditory prompts to aid in communication skills development.

Speech and Language Therapy

Interactive exercises to improve speech articulation and language comprehension. Visual and auditory prompts to aid in communication skills development

Cognitive and Academic Skill Development

Engaging activities focusing on math, reading, writing, and other academic subjects. Memory games, puzzles, and problem-solving exercises to enhance cognitive abilities.

Visual and Auditory Support:

High-contrast visuals, large fonts, and customizable backgrounds for visually impaired children. Clear, simple auditory instructions and feedback for children with hearing impairments.
